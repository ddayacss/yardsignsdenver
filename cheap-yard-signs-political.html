Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my coroplast sign order?

You can customize your coroplast sign by selecting its size, design/color, and text. Some suppliers even allow you to upload your own design.

How long does it take to produce a custom coroplast sign?

The production time varies per supplier and order quantity. However, the majority of orders are ready within 3-7 business days after design approval.

Are there any restrictions on what can be printed on a custom coroplast sign?

Generally, as long as the image does not violate copyright laws and comply with decency standards, there should be no restrictions on the design printed.

Is there a minimum order quantity for custom coroplast signs?

Usually, there isn't a minimum order required for making custom coroplast signs.

Are the custom coroplast signs weatherproof?

Yes, coroplast signs are weatherproof and are designed to withstand various environmental conditions.

What size options are available for custom coroplast signs?

Size options would typically depend on the supplier, but generally, they range from small sizes ideal for yard signs to larger sizes for billboard-style displays.
