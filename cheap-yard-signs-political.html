Are there any restrictions on what can be printed on a custom coroplast sign?

Generally, as long as the image does not violate copyright laws and comply with decency standards, there should be no restrictions on the design printed.

Can I place an order for custom coroplast signs online?

Yes, most suppliers allow online ordering for custom coroplast signs.

Can the colors on the custom coroplast signs fade over time?

With proper care and limited exposure to direct sunlight, the colors would not fade quickly on a custom coroplast sign.

Is there a minimum order quantity for custom coroplast signs?

Usually, there isn't a minimum order required for making custom coroplast signs.

Can I use my own design for a custom coroplast sign?

Yes, most suppliers allow customers to use their own designs for custom coroplast signs.

What is the material used in making coroplast signs?

Coroplast signs are made from corrugated plastic which is lightweight yet durable.

Can I purchase double-sided custom coroplast signs?

Yes, many suppliers offer double-sided printing for coroplast signs.
