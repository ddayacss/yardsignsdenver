Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use my own design for a custom coroplast sign?

Yes, most suppliers allow customers to use their own designs for custom coroplast signs.

What type of images or files can I use for my custom design?

Most suppliers accept JPEG, PNG, PDF, and other common design files types. However, it's best to check each supplier's specific requirements.

Can the colors on the custom coroplast signs fade over time?

With proper care and limited exposure to direct sunlight, the colors would not fade quickly on a custom coroplast sign.

What are the payment methods accepted for purchasing the lawn care signs?

Installation methods may vary depending on the size and intended location of the sign. Some common methods include using step stakes for lawn placements or hanging with rope or bungee cords for larger signs. Always follow the instruction provided by your supplier for correct installation.

What type of finish will my custom coroplast sign have?

Most custom coroplast signs have a semi-gloss finish, but the choice varies depending on the supplier.

What is the material used in making coroplast signs?

Coroplast signs are made from corrugated plastic which is lightweight yet durable.

What is a coroplast sign?

A coroplast sign is a lightweight and durable signage solution made from corrugated plastic, often used for outdoor advertising or informational notices.
