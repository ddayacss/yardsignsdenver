A coroplast sign is a lightweight and durable signage solution made from corrugated plastic, often used for outdoor advertising or informational notices.

Yes, most suppliers allow online ordering for custom coroplast signs.

Size options would typically depend on the supplier, but generally, they range from small sizes ideal for yard signs to larger sizes for billboard-style displays.

You can customize your coroplast sign by selecting its size, design/color, and text. Some suppliers even allow you to upload your own design.

Yes, coroplast signs are weatherproof and are designed to withstand various environmental conditions.
