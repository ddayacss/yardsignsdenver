Frequently Asked Questions

What type of images or files can I use for my custom design?

Most suppliers accept JPEG, PNG, PDF, and other common design files types. However, it's best to check each supplier's specific requirements.

How do I customize my coroplast sign order?

You can customize your coroplast sign by selecting its size, design/color, and text. Some suppliers even allow you to upload your own design.

How long does it take to produce a custom coroplast sign?

The production time varies per supplier and order quantity. However, the majority of orders are ready within 3-7 business days after design approval.

What is a coroplast sign?

A coroplast sign is a lightweight and durable signage solution made from corrugated plastic, often used for outdoor advertising or informational notices.

Are the custom coroplast signs weatherproof?

Yes, coroplast signs are weatherproof and are designed to withstand various environmental conditions.
